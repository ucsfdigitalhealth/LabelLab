Access to the Dataset

To access the dataset, please fill in the following form: Dataset Access Form. Once the form is submitted, you will receive a link to download the dataset. Please note that it is essential to provide proper acknowledgment to the original authors and cite the following paper in your research appropriately.

Qian, Yang, Yinan Sun, Ali Kargarandehkordi, Onur Cezmi Mutlu, Saimourya Surabhi, Pingyi Chen, Zain Jabbar, Dennis Paul Wall, and Peter Washington. "TikTokActions: A TikTok-Derived Video Dataset for Human Action Recognition." arXiv preprint arXiv:2402.08875 (2024).
